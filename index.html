Why coding?

I love a good challenge! Growing up, I couldn't get enough of puzzles, riddles and thinking outside the box. Fast forward to the real world and a quick few years at a boring desk job made me realize I didn't want to waste my work life being uninspired.

After a few months spent completing Tech Talent South's Coding Immersion Bootcamp, I've finally found that challenging and inspiring atmosphere I loved as a child -- the world of development!
